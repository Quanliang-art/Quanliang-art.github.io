This website features images, articles, and videos to urge modern individuals to protect the Galapagos tortoises.

This website features images, articles, and videos to urge modern individuals to protect the Galapagos tortoises. From the 16th to the 19th century, human activities inflicted devastating blows on the populations of these tortoises. However, since the 19th century, people have become aware of their mistakes and have begun efforts to save these rare animals. The website uses two timelines as visual guides to illustrate the long and arduous survival journey of the Galapagos tortoises under human impact. It also highlights the shift in human attitudes towards nature, from conquest and destruction to coexistence and protection.
